Biography

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a major role in India's missile and space programs and inspired millions of students through his speeches and books.

Timeline

  • 1931 - Born in Rameshwaram
  • 1958 – Studied Aeronautical Engineering
  • 1980 – SLV-3 Launch Success
  • 1998 – Pokhran-II
  • 2002 – Became President of India
  • 2015 – Passed away at IIM Shillong
